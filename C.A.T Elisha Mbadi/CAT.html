Sun and Surf is a project started by Matt and Andrew Lorem We opened our shores in 2013 and have never looked back. Engage with us today and get a new lease of life, and a bit of seawater in your face. We have three kinds of watersports:

Scuba Diving

Have you ever wondered what fish look like deep in the ocean or what it feels like to swim 50feet under water?

With our scuba diving classes, you can finally feel what it's like to swim with the fishes.Hopefully, you'll even make a friend.

Kite Surfing

Kite surfing is one of the most enjoyed activities of all our catalogue, and its easy to learn as well.

It's about picking up your kite and letting the winds carry you to eternity. Come join us today and chart your course.

Sailing Boats

Ever wanted to feel what Jack Sparrow felt like as he captained his ship in the high seas? We can put you there.

Whether you want to sail a dinghy and feel the serenity of the calm sea or captain a catamaran with your crew, we are your best shot.
